# Content Structure Templates

Compact blueprints for common SEO formats. Replace every placeholder, keep the **primary keyword in the H1 and first 100 words**, and remove sections that do not match search intent.

## Shared Build Rules

| Element | Include |
|---------|---------|
| Opening | Hook, problem, promise, primary keyword early |
| Proof | Data, examples, quotes, first-hand experience, or source/date citations |
| Structure | Clear H2/H3 hierarchy, short paragraphs, tables only for decisions |
| GEO | 40-60 word definition block, quotable takeaways, FAQ for long-tail retrieval |
| Links | 3-5 internal links and 2-3 authoritative external links unless the format needs more |
| Ending | Recap, recommendation, and one next step or CTA |

## Blueprint Matrix

| Format | Word count | Goal | Required sections |
|--------|------------|------|-------------------|
| Blog post | 1,200-1,800 | Educate and rank for informational queries | H1 with benefit, definition, why it matters, 2+ main sections, common mistakes, FAQ, conclusion |
| Comparison article | 1,500-2,500 | Help the reader choose | Quick verdict, comparison table, Option A/B summaries, detailed category comparison, pricing, who should choose each, final verdict |
| Listicle | 1,500-2,500 | Rank for `best [topic]` queries | Quick summary table, evaluation criteria, repeated item cards, comparison table, how to choose, top pick |
| How-to guide | 1,200-2,000 | Teach a process | Tools/time/skill level, numbered steps, examples, troubleshooting, FAQ, next step |
| Product review | 1,500-2,000 | Help buyers decide | Testing context, overview, pros/cons, key features, performance testing, pricing, competitor comparison, buyer fit, verdict |
| Pillar page | 3,000-5,000+ | Serve as definitive cluster hub | TOC, fundamentals, major subtopics, advanced tactics, implementation/case studies, FAQ, takeaways, resources |
| FAQ page | 1,000-1,500 | Rank for question queries and support FAQ rich results | Intro, grouped questions, direct answers, comparison/pricing questions if relevant, advanced questions, CTA |

## Copy-Start Patterns

### Blog Post

```markdown
# [Primary Keyword]: [Benefit or Hook]

[Hook] [Problem] [Promise]

## What Is [Topic]?
[40-60 word definition]

## Why It Matters
- [Benefit]
- [Benefit]
> **Key insight**: [dated stat or quotable point]

## [Main Section]
[Explanation + example]

## Common Mistakes
### [Mistake]
[Why it hurts and how to avoid it]

## FAQ
### [Question]?
[40-60 word answer]

## Conclusion
[Recap + CTA]
```

### Comparison Article

```markdown
# [Option A] vs [Option B]: Which Is Better for [Use Case]?

**Quick answer**: [one-line verdict]

| Feature | [Option A] | [Option B] |
|---------|------------|------------|
| Best for | [use case] | [use case] |
| Starting price | [price + source/date] | [price + source/date] |

## Detailed Comparison
## What Is [Option A]?
**Pros**: [pros]
**Cons**: [cons]

## What Is [Option B]?
**Pros**: [pros]
**Cons**: [cons]

### [Category]
**Winner**: [A/B/Tie] — [why]

## Pricing
| Plan | [Option A] | [Option B] |
|------|------------|------------|
| [tier] | [price + source/date] | [price + source/date] |

## Which Should You Choose?
**Choose [A] if**: [criteria]
**Choose [B] if**: [criteria]

## Final Verdict
[balanced recommendation]
```

### Listicle

```markdown
# [Number] Best [Items] for [Audience/Purpose] ([Year])

## Quick Summary
| Rank | Item | Best for | Price |
|------|------|----------|-------|
| 1 | [item] | [use case] | [price + source/date] |

## How We Chose
- [criterion]
- [criterion]

## 1. [Item] — Best for [Use Case]
**What it is**: [description]
**Pros**: [pros]
**Cons**: [cons]
**Best for**: [audience]

Repeat for items `2-N`, then add comparison table and top-pick conclusion.
```

### How-To Guide

```markdown
# How to [Achieve Goal]: [Number]-Step Guide [for Audience]

## What You'll Need
**Tools**: [tools] | **Time**: [estimate] | **Skill level**: [level]

## Step 1: [Action]
1. [Sub-step]
2. [Sub-step]
> **Pro tip**: [shortcut or caution]

Repeat until final step, then add troubleshooting, FAQ, and next step.
```

### Product Review

```markdown
# [Product] Review: Is It Worth It in [Year]?

## Overview
**What it is**: [description]
**Price**: [details + source/date]
**Best for**: [use case]
**Rating**: [score or verdict]

## Performance Testing
**Method**: [method]
**Results**: [data]
**Verdict**: [assessment]

## Pros and Cons
**Pros**: [pros]
**Cons**: [cons]

## Key Features
### [Feature]
[experience + evidence]

## Pricing and Competitors
| Feature | [Product] | [Competitor 1] | [Competitor 2] |
|---------|-----------|----------------|----------------|
| [factor] | [details] | [details] | [details] |

## Who Should Buy It?
**Perfect for**: [audience]
**Not ideal for**: [audience]

## Final Verdict
[recommendation by use case]
```

### Pillar Page

```markdown
# [Topic]: The Complete Guide [for Audience] ([Year])

## Table of Contents
[Chapters 1-N]

## Chapter 1: Fundamentals
### What Is [Topic]?
[definition]
### Key Concepts
| Term | Definition |
|------|------------|
| [term] | [definition] |

## Chapter 2-N: [Major Subtopics]
[deep dives, frameworks, examples]

## FAQ
[8-10 questions]

## Resources
**Recommended tools**: [tools]
**Further reading**: [internal + external links]
```

### FAQ Page

```markdown
# [Topic]: Frequently Asked Questions

## General Questions
### What is [topic]?
[direct answer]

## Getting Started
### How do I get started with [topic]?
[direct answer]

## Common Problems
### Why isn't [result] happening?
[direct answer]

## Comparison / Pricing
### [Option A] vs [Option B]: which is better?
[direct answer]

## Advanced Questions
### Can I [advanced action]?
[direct answer]

## Still Have Questions?
[CTA]
```

## Implementation Checklist

- [ ] Replace placeholders and remove irrelevant sections.
- [ ] Put the primary keyword in H1 and first 100 words.
- [ ] Use secondary keywords naturally in H2/H3s.
- [ ] Add proof, examples, source/date citations, or data.
- [ ] Include FAQ and definition blocks when useful for GEO retrieval.
- [ ] Add internal/external links and descriptive image alt text.
- [ ] Include affiliate disclosure when applicable.
- [ ] Finish with meta title and description matching the final angle.
